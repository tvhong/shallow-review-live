Hackathon Project

STEER

Safety Talent Entry and Education Router

An AI-powered career advisor that gives personalized, actionable guidance to people entering AI safety — built with curated resources and eval-driven prompt engineering.

The Problem

AI safety needs people. But getting started is overwhelming.

  • Dozens of courses, fellowships, orgs, and career paths — it can be overwhelming to navigate alone
  • Talking to an advisor helps — but slots are limited and take time to book
  • What if you just want a low-commitment way to start exploring right now?

Our Approach

A Discord / Slack chatbot for AI safety career advice

  • Chatbot — available 24/7 on Discord, gives personalized guidance in a conversation
  • Intake form — structured data collection up front, no slow back-and-forth
  • Curated prompt — 40+ vetted resources, research fellowships, career roadmaps
  • Feedback pipeline — real user interactions feed back to improve the prompt; starting with evals while we build up data
